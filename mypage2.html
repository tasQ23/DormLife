During this time, it is normal for students to get tempted to eat out or bring take outs.
This can be due to lack of time to cook or because they are tired of whole day's work and do not have the energy to cook.
So eating out or take outs seem logical at that time, but it may seem the quickest way to satisfy you stomach but not only you are spending
unnecessarily, you are also treating yourself unhealthy food which will affect you in the long run.
It is best to maintain a fixed routine where you have set at least 15 minutes to make something for yourself,
not only you are eating home-made healthy food but you are also taking a break.
This short break while you are cooking will divert your mind, make your stomach happy and you can go back
to your work happily and can concentrate more.
